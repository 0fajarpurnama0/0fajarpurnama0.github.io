## Chapter 1.1 Orientation

[Location: The Front Gates of Cross Academy - Early Evening]

Fajar stepped out of the portal, and reality snapped into place around him. The air was crisp, carrying the scent of pine trees and an underlying, faint metallic tang that he would soon learn was the ambient magic of the area.

He stood before the massive, towering wrought-iron gates of Cross Academy. Beyond the gates, the sprawling, European-style campus looked like a fortress. The sun was just beginning to set, casting long, dramatic shadows across the cobblestone path.

He looked down at his hands. They were smooth, lacking the heavy calluses of a seasoned martial artist. His muscles were standard—healthy, but not yet forged by the fires of Phase I. He was completely empty-handed, save for a standard, black Day Class uniform neatly folded in a standard-issue duffel bag Gemini had manifested at his feet upon entry, containing only his school ID and dormitory keys.

"You're the new transfer student?" the girl asked, stepping forward with a welcoming, albeit cautious, smile. "I'm Yuki Cross. This is Zero Kiryu. We're the school Guardians. Headmaster Cross sent us to escort you to your dorm."

Zero's eyes narrowed, sweeping over Fajar's relaxed, entirely unguarded posture. To Zero's hyper-tuned Hunter senses, Fajar registered as an absolute zero—no magical aura, no threat, just an oblivious, defenceless human walking into a den of monsters.

A cluttered room bred a cluttered mind, and Fajar needed his mind to be a flawless mirror.

With the room secured and pristine, he stripped down to his undershirt and sweatpants.

He began with Tier 1: Bio-Maintenance. Fajar started from his neck, slowly rolling his joints and working his way down to his ankles. My mind is ten times faster than my body, Fajar analyzed, feeling the slight lag between his brain's command and his arm's execution.
